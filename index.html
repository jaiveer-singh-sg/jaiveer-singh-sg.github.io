04 — Buyer Checklist

What regulated buyers should verify

01

Compliance posture

SOC 2 Type II as a baseline; alignment with MAS TRM, GLBA, NYDFS, or the EU AI Act where applicable.

02

Workflow scope

Point solutions plateau — platforms that orchestrate intake through compliance compound value faster.

03

Integration depth

Value depends on connection to core banking, claims, and CRM systems — not the model alone.

04

Evidence, not logos

Ask for production references in your exact vertical and institution size, not a marketing case study.
